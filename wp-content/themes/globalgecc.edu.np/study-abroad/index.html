  • Japan

    Japan – land of Rising Sun is a country with rich nature, diverse topography, and beautiful turns of the seasons. Japan has always been one of the favorite destinations for students when it comes to abroad studies. Foreign students in Japan are provided with work permit enabling them to earn-as-you-learn. Japan, also known as ‘Nippon’ in local language, is a land of endless opportunities. It is a home to some of the world most trusted brands, vehicles and machinery like that of cars, motorbikes and electronic gadgets, including Honda, Nissan, Toyota, Yamaha and Sony. Success of such large fleet of products undoubtedly goes to the quality education and respect for work culture rooted in Japanese people.

    Why study In Japan?

    • Japan is one of the safest countries in the world where students get a peaceful place to study. Japan also has one of the world’s most advanced healthcare systems, reflected in this high life expectancy.
    • International students can discover Japan’s rich cultural heritage. Japan has a long and fascinating history filled war and intrigue. Students can find out peaceful temples and shrines, demon museums, and the history of how Japan was unified by the sword.
    • Tuition fees are comparatively cheap in Japan which is one of the best options to get highly qualified education in convenient way. A wide range of other scholarships are available, with some directed towards specific nationalities, women, minorities, or those with high level Japanese proficiency.
    • International students get high educational standards and excellent research environment. Many institutions of higher education, such as universities and junior colleges, are well equipped with fine research, computer, and library facilities and enable students to carry out their research in an excellent environment.
    • Universities of Japan are recognized as the leading research universities in the world and at the forefront of technological innovation in Japan. The strong orientation toward innovation, partnership with the local community and industry, and pioneering efforts in interdisciplinary research and education, all reflect the university’s unique history and characteristics.

  • Newzealand

    New Zealand is a country in the South western Pacific Ocean consisting of 2 main island, both marked by volcanoes and glaciations.

    The education system in New Zealand is a three tier model which includes primary and intermediate school followed by secondary school (high school) and tertiary education at university. New Zealand education system allows for flexibility choice and great support. New Zealand welcomes international students from all around the globe. New Zealand qualification are world –class and have a number of system in place to make sure international students are looked after well.

    Why study in New Zealand?

    • New Zealand offers a world class education in a postcard- perfect environment. The New Zealand Qualification Authority (NZQA) requires that institution are registered to enrol international student and ensures compliance with qualification standard.
    • Five New Zealand universities were ranked in the top 400 universities in the world by the 2013-2014 times higher education world university ranking.
    • New Zealand has always been internationally recognized for its excellent education standards and for training high quality teachers.
    • Well known and trusted for its quality education.
    • Degree and education programme are based on the British Education system.
    • Competitive tuition fees and lower cost of living.
    • Easy access to one of the world most beautiful natural environment.
    • Safe study destination with zero tolerance towards racism.
    • A country of multi-ethnic society that welcomes cultures from all over the world.
    • Option to work part time both on campus and off campus without any restriction on the type of job you do in part time role.

  • Bangladesh

    Bangladesh is known as one of the peaceful countries in the world. Bangladesh is very popular for MBBS and BDS programs among Nepalese students. It has become the best destination away from home for medical students to achieve a quality medical education. Colleges of Bangladesh are under the Jurisdiction of the Ministry of Health and Family welfare. Most of the colleges in Bangladesh are recognized by Nepal Medical Council(NMC),Medical Council of India and listed in WHO and IME. The degree of colleges is universally accepted .The pass out result from Nepal Medical Council licensing examination is remarkable as compared with other countries.

    Why Bangladesh as your Medical destination?

    • Affordable tuition fee- Easy instalment basis.
    • The colleges recognized by NMC, BMDC, South East Asia and MCI. Enlisted in WHO and IME.
    • Book , Syllabus, Teaching and learning methodology are similar to Nepal with Excellent academic environment.
    • College with own multi-specialty hospital with good number of patient flow.
    • Living expenses are same as Nepal
    • Separate hostel for boys and girls.
    • Graduate students are eligible to appear in United States Medical Licensing Examination(USMLE)
